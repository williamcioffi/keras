isotope explorer

To start using the isotope explorer select one or more animals from animal_id. You can change the type of plot from isospace to a timeseries for either carbon or nitrogen isotope values where the x axis is the position the sample was taken along the plate. Closer to the root is to the left and indicates the most recent material and as you move to the right on the x axis you are moving back in time through the whales life toward the distal tip of the plate.

abbreviations:
  • bp[01..15] = Balaenoptera physalus samples #1-15.
  • eg[01] = Eubalaena glacialis sample #1.
  • c-n-isospace = carbon and nitrogen biplot.
  • time-d13c = δ13C (permil) plotted against position along growth axis (cm).
  • time-d15n = δ15N (permil) plotted against plosition along growth axis (cm).
